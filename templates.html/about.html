About Us

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Lagos, Ace Books stands as a haven
for bibliophiles and literary enthusiasts alike.
Established in the year 2020, this literary gem emerged from the
collective passion for the written word shared by a group of eight friends.
More than just a bookstore, Ace Books is a testament to the power of dreams,
offering a welcoming space where seekers of new adventures,
moments of quiet reflection, and connections with fellow book lovers converge.
In the midst of the bustling city, Ace Books beckons as a destination where dreams
are sparked, imaginations are kindled, and stories come to life, creating
a unique and enriching experience for all who step through its doors.
